3D Methods is the independent studio of 3D artist and game developer, Jacob A. Medina. Born, raised, and living in Southern California. He began working with computer graphics as far back as 2004 using software such as Vue and Bryce. He was originally inspired to create mobile games by playing the original Game Boy as a child.

He began mobile game development in 2011 and released his first game, Cryptic Keep, in July of 2011 on the Apple app store. From there he developed five additional mobile games that would jumpstart his career into the game industry.
